BLACKSMITHING

We do a variety of metalworking at Vlybrook. Tools, Art, Hooks, and more. You name it, we can probably smith it. We take pride in the quality of our metalworking. We hope that you will too.

LUMBER

In an old sawmill from another era rests a gigantic bandsaw. With it, we preserve tradition, and create only the finest antique boards and grass rakes. The machine itself is a spectacle to watch, to say the least.

TRADITION

Nothing suggests antiquity and tradition quite like a rustic blacksmith's shop. It's our opinion that while a clean and pristine workspace is nice, nothing beats the genuinity of a well-worked environment.
